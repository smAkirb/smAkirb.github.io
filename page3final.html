About

In the Medical part of the world Augmented Reality has become a huge part in helping people that need the assistance. A good example would be helping to train new nurses with dummys instead of actual patients. It can also help some affected by partial blindness. It can help nurses & doctors alike both with learning the human body and experimenting with what might go wrong during a treatment. Overall there is no limit for what Augmented Reality can do in the medical field for both patients and health providers alike.
